About this site

Discrimination and racism is a very delicate issue. Each person's perception and way of thinking is different. When discussing the topic of discrimination and racism, it is important to learn and know first before giving your opinion.

Our team consists of students from different schools.

2 of our members attend an international school, while the other attends a Japanese prefectural high school.

Because our team members consists of students from different schools, we were able to compare how our awareness of discrimination differs. We were able to learn how much Japanese high school students understood the topic of discrimination, as well as the difference in awareness between international school students that have experienced discrimination.

Through this site, we would like more high school students to understand the issue of discrimination.
