About Discrimination


Discrimination is an issue that involves the whole world. Discrimination is not an issue that we have nothing to do with. In 2020, racist incidents in the United States intensified the opposition movement once again, and the world began to focus on the issue of discrimination. This site details the history of racism against black people in the United States, organizations campaigning against racism in the United States, and other forms of discrimination occurring around the world. This is your opportunity to once again ask yourself, "what is discrimination" on this site.

    About Discrimination

    The purpose of this website is to point out the importance of understanding the topic of discrimination (targeted specifically for high school students).

    One of the reasons we emphasize the importance of understanding is based on our own experience. Two of our team members attend an international school (hereinafter referred to as "inter"), and many of our friends have experienced discrimination. Discrimination is also a topic that is often brought up in our lessons.

    However, as we conducted deeper research into the topic of discrimination, we realized how much we lacked in understanding the topic of discrimination. This made us realize that even people who think they are aware of this issue can lack understanding, even when they are concerned with it. We realized that the level of concern does not equal the level of understanding which further emphasizes our point.

    Even so, there are many people around us who express their opinions without understanding the issue of discrimination. Discrimination is not an issue to be dismissed with "It doesn’t concern/involve me", but is neither an issue where people can state opinions without understanding or with the flow. We want to prevent people from stating opinions regarding the issue of discrimination as it creates misunderstandings amongst the people stating their opinions as well as their peers, which could contribute to the spread of false information.

    This site is not meant to state our opinions regarding the issue of discrimination, but rather the importance of understanding the issue. We hope this site gives a clearer insight into the issue of discrimination and reflect on whether you truly understand this issue.


    Focus

    For our research topic, we focused on black discrimination in the United States. Of course, there are several other types of discrimination that is occurring in several other countries besides the United States. Our purpose to focus on one country and one type of discrimination is to provide in-depth research to the viewers regarding the issue of discrimination. By focusing on a specific scale, it also allows the website to be a good opportunity for readers to research further on other types of discrimination. Thus we decided to make our focus on the research on racism in the United States.
